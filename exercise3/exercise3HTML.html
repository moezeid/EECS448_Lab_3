Kanye West


Kanye West is a hip hop artist, fashion designer, and public icon.
From humble beginnings as a beat producer in the late 90s, Kanye has worked his way to the top of the rap industry.
His first project, "The College Dropout" received critical acclaim and kickstarted his career as a rapper.
Other famous works include "Graduation," "Yeezus," "Late Registration," and more.
